SK Hospital,

As one of the foremost names in healthcare names in the region SK Hospital has been setting standards since its inception in April 2009. The modern State of the art 300 bedded hospital has the most eminent panel of medical professionals, a well trained paramedical staff and the most skilled and caring nursing professionals.

Backing them is the best healthcare infrastructure and the latest state of the art equipments. We have operation Theatre with Laminar flow, state - of - the - Art Digital Flat Panel Cath Lab, Burn Unit, Intensive Care Unit, NICU and PICU for Neonates, Ultra modern labs for the investigations and fully ventilated ambulance available 24 hours. Our Team puts in the best efforts to provide quality patient care and will always continue to take that extra step to meet the needs and expectations of all who walk in to our facility.

  • SPECALITY : SK Hospital is a centre of excellence in Cardiac Care providing world class care to patients. The team of doctors, nurses and paramedical staff work together to ensure the best healthcare delivery to patients. Our team of Cardiology is available round the clock to help patients with cardiac emergencies. The centre performs all kind of Interventional procedures and Cardiac surgeries.
  • MISSION : Committed with patients & public at large for all aspects, be its preventive, curative or palliative in nature.
